Social Media

Social media platforms provide young people with a range of benefits to empower them in a variety of ways. These benefits include:

  • Helping people communicate
  • Collaborating
  • Sharing ideas and experiences

    • Like any form of social engagement, social media also comes with risks such as:

  • Disclosing personal information
  • Cyberbullying
  • Scams

    • Video Gaming

    Humans are playful creatures. Video gaming, like most forms of play, provides achievements, rewards, competition and social engagement in a fun way. Some benefits of gaming include:

  • A fun hobby
  • Playing with friends
  • Experiencing an interactive story
  • Talking with friends online

    • As gaming becomes more popular, the risks and dangers associated with gaming have become more noticable. These include:

  • Spending too much real money on microtransactions
  • Gaming addiction
  • Cyberbullying in an online video game
