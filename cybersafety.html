Cybersafety

What is Bullying and Harassment?

This is any ongoing or targeted behaviour, which may cause embarassment, fear, humiliation or feeling uncomfortable - because of someone's deliberately hurtful actions or words.

It can be expressed physically, verbally or electronically - through social media.


Harassment

Harassment is any verbal, physical or sexual conduct which is uninvited, unwelcome and which is reasonably likely to humiliate, offend, intimidate or distress someone.
